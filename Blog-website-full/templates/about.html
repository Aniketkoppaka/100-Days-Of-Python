About Me

This is what I do.

Hi, I’m Aniket, a computer science student with a strong interest in software development, automation, and real-world problem solving. I’m passionate about building clean, functional applications using tools like Python, Flask, and APIs. I enjoy breaking down problems, writing efficient code, and turning ideas into working projects.

A lot of my time goes into experimenting with backend logic, web automation (like Selenium), and improving my understanding of how different technologies interact. I like working on things that are both practical and challenging whether that’s a quiz app, a flight deal tracker, or something completely new.

Beyond code, I’m someone who learns by building. I’m always looking for ways to grow whether it’s picking up a new library, debugging smarter, or learning from others. Tech moves fast, and I enjoy keeping pace with it while creating things that are useful and meaningful.
